About Us

Meet the EDwareness team


EDwareness is created by a group of Year 3 Nursing students from Nanyang Polytechnic (NYP) Singapore.


The word “EDwareness” comes from Eating Disorder Awareness. After conducting surveys and interviews of more than 100 participants, we decided to create this website as there is still little knowledge from the public regarding the awareness of anorexia nervosa. Therefore, we have developed this website to increase the awareness of anorexia nervosa.
